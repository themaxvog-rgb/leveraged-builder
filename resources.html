YouTube Video Script Generator
↓

Use with Claude. Replace brackets with your specifics.

You are a world-class YouTube scriptwriter specializing in AI entrepreneurship content. Write a [8-12 minute] video script on: [TOPIC] Audience: Ambitious 25-35 year olds who want to build leveraged businesses using AI. They're smart, skeptical of hype, and respond to systems and real numbers. Requirements: - Hook must create curiosity or contrast in the first 5 seconds - Use the contrast storytelling framework (before → discovery → after) - Include 3 concrete, numbered frameworks or steps - Add 2-3 pattern interrupt moments (surprising stat, bold claim, or counter-intuitive insight) - End with a specific CTA to subscribe + join the newsletter Tone: Direct, peer-level, confident. No filler phrases. No "in this video I will show you." Output: Full script with [B-ROLL] cues and timestamps.
Short-form Hook Generator (20 variations)
↓
Generate 20 hooks for a short-form video about: [TOPIC] Target audience: Ambitious 25-35 year olds building online businesses. Mix these formats evenly: - Curiosity gap ("Nobody talks about...") - Contrast ("Before AI: X. After AI: Y.") - Identity ("If you're still doing X...") - Data/proof ("I tested X for 30 days...") - Urgency ("The window for X is closing...") Rules: - Each hook must be under 12 words - No generic phrases ("in this video", "today I'm going to") - Each must make the viewer feel they'll miss something if they scroll - No questions that can be answered with yes/no Output as a numbered list with the hook type labeled.
Newsletter Weekly Brief Writer
↓
Write a premium newsletter issue for The Leveraged Builder. Topic this week: [TOPIC] Format: 1. Subject line (5 options — curiosity, contrast, data, identity, utility) 2. Opening hook (2-3 sentences, no greeting) 3. The Big Idea (150 words — the core insight of this issue) 4. Three Takeaways (bullet format, each actionable in under 1 hour) 5. One Tool Spotlight (name, what it does, best use case, link) 6. One number (a stat, metric or data point that reframes the topic) 7. Closing line (1 sentence — not a summary, a provocation) Tone: Premium intelligence briefing. Peer-level. Direct. No filler. Length: 400-550 words total. Voice: The Leveraged Builder — systems-oriented, honest, ambitious.
Twitter/X Thread Generator
↓
Write a high-engagement Twitter/X thread about: [TOPIC] Structure: - Tweet 1: Hook (bold claim or surprising contrast, under 200 chars) - Tweets 2-8: The breakdown (one point per tweet, each self-contained) - Tweet 9: The lesson/framework (the distilled takeaway) - Tweet 10: CTA (follow + newsletter, natural not forced) Rules: - Every tweet must be able to stand alone - Use line breaks for readability (max 2-3 lines per tweet) - Include at least 1 specific number or data point - No hashtags - No emojis unless they replace a word - End tweet 1 with "🧵" only Tone: Direct, peer-level. Like a smart friend sharing what they learned. Topic context: AI entrepreneurship, leveraged business building.
Strategy Content Repurposing Engine
