About this project

Why The Leveraged Builder exists.

This isn't a personal brand. It's a thesis — tested in public, documented weekly, built to prove that AI changes everything for independent builders.

The problem we're solving

Most business content falls into two traps. Either it's generic motivation that tells you to "work harder" — or it's tech hype that tells you AI will do everything without showing you how. Neither is useful. Neither shows you the actual system.

What this actually is

The Leveraged Builder is a media company built on one bet: the next generation of successful entrepreneurs will be AI-native operators. Not people who use AI as a shortcut, but people who build their entire business infrastructure around it.

Every week, we document what that looks like in practice. Real tools. Real workflows. Real numbers from a business being built in public — from São Paulo, for a global audience.

Who this is for

You're ambitious. You're already working — maybe in a job you want to escape, maybe in a freelance career that's hit a ceiling, maybe in an early-stage business that's not moving fast enough. You know AI is changing the rules. You just want someone to show you exactly how to use it to build something real.

What we believe

  • 01
    AI is infrastructure, not a shortcut The people winning with AI aren't using it to do tasks faster. They're using it to build systems that eliminate the tasks entirely.
  • 02
    Systems beat hustle. Always. A well-designed system running 24/7 produces more than any individual effort. The goal is to become the architect, not the worker.
  • 03
    Location is no longer a constraint The best opportunities are borderless. Where you live should determine your lifestyle, not your income ceiling.
  • 04
    Transparency builds better audiences The most valuable content is the one that shows the real process — including the mistakes. We build in public because it's more useful and more honest.
  • 05
    Small teams are a competitive advantage With AI, a two-person operation can outperform a ten-person team. Lean is fast. Fast wins.
